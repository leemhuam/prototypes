How do I summon Lowebot?


Lowebot can be summoned to your every need by either pressing the round summon button in the bottom right or by clicking the hamburger menu button and tapping "Summon Robot"


How do I talk to Lowebot?


Lowebot can understand spoken language.  Simply press the "Talk with Lowebot" button on Lowebot's front screen and speak like you normally would to to a person.  Lowebot will show an image of the item for you to confirm.  Once confirmed, simply follow Lowebot to where you can pick that item up.


How do I take pictures?


Using the My Lil' Lowebot app, you can take a picture of any tool or item such as screws, pipes, and drills to quickly find what you're looking for in our store.  To do so, simply click the camera button in the top right hand corner, place the item in front of a flat, light colored background, and take a picture.  The item will automatically be added to Lowebot's list of things to get.


How do I call

for a professional?


Lowebot can answer many questions about the location of items in the store.  For more detailed questions, Lowebot can automatically call a professional via video chat for immediate help.  To do this, press the "Speak to a Professional" button on Lowebot's screen.

do if robots are taking

all their jobs?


Lowebot is here to free up our employees so they can focus on other customer's needs.  Lowebot is able to facilitate the transmission of expertise from our professionals in all departments with you the customer by allowing you to video chat at any time.
