About Us

Formed in 1902, our Japanese Language School is our signature program for the teaching of Japanese to children and adults. JLS not only offers excellent language education but also provides its students with a variety of cultural learning opportunities not available at other Japanese language schools. We teach a Fall and Spring semester with classes meeting once a week for 12 weeks.

Who We Are

The JLS is the oldest operating Japanese language school in the continental United States with over one hundred years of history. First founded in 1902, the JLS has grown with the community, changing and adapting to meet community needs, yet staying true to its mission of providing Japanese language and cultural education.

JLS offers both adult evening and youth weekend classes. Please visit the course description and class schedule pages to find the class that will work for you.
